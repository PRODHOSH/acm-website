Event Report

How The Event Went

A complete day-wise summary from registration to closing ceremony.

Server Surfers 2026 commenced at 9:30 AM with registration and check-in at VOC Auditorium, where participants received event kits, completed onboarding, and settled in. At 10:00 AM, the inauguration and welcome address by ACM chapter leads and faculty coordinators formally opened the event.

At 10:15 AM, Mr. Arun Gantayat (Kaspersky India) delivered the keynote session, sharing insights on real-world cybersecurity threats, enterprise security posture, and current trends in the evolving threat landscape, followed by an interactive Q&A. At 10:30 AM, the Kaspersky Interactive Protection Simulation (KIPS) began, engaging teams in a strategy-based cyber defense experience.

The KIPS segment was conducted in three structured phases: 1) Game Rules and Housekeeping (20 minutes), 2) Team Strategy Simulation (120 minutes), and 3) Debrief and Lessons Learned (25 minutes). During this session, teams analyzed threat news, selected defense cards, managed budgets, and tracked their performance on the live leaderboard. A lunch break at 1:15 PM offered networking time before the competition segment.

The Capture The Flag (CTF) competition started at 2:30 PM and ran for 120 minutes, where participants solved cybersecurity challenges by applying practical skills in a live competitive setting. The day concluded at 4:30 PM with results, awards, and closing remarks, recognizing top teams and highlighting key takeaways from both simulation and CTF rounds.

Total runtime: 9:30 AM - 5:30 PM | High engagement across keynote, KIPS, and CTF.
